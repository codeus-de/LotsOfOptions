About This Tool

A simple visualization tool for cash-secured put options.

What are Cash-Secured Puts?

A cash-secured put is an options strategy where you sell a put option and set aside enough cash to buy the stock if the option is assigned. This strategy is used when you're bullish or neutral on a stock and wouldn't mind owning it at a lower price.

What This Tool Does

This simple visualizer allows you to see the potential outcomes of your cash-secured put strategy at expiration. You can visualize your maximum profit (the premium received), maximum loss, and break-even point.
